Recommendation — Based on Your Non-Negotiables

Occidental Tamarindo wins on nightlife — the only one where you can walk out the front gate and be in a real town with bars, live music, and people. It's also the most affordable. The tradeoffs are real: food is the weakest, excursions cost extra, and housekeeping has been flagged in recent reviews.

Planet Hollywood wins on housekeeping, food variety, and excursion access — but it's isolated on the Papagayo Peninsula with no town to explore. If the group is happy staying on-property in the evenings, the 8-restaurant spread and Nomad Tours partnership make it the most seamless all-inclusive experience. Closest to the airport too.

Azura is the most intimate — adults-only with a boutique feel, solid food, and good activities — but Sámara's nightlife is the quietest of the three areas and it's the furthest drive from LIR.

If nightlife is non-negotiable → Occidental Tamarindo. If quality and seamlessness are the priority → Planet Hollywood. If boutique and adults-only matters most → Azura.

Excursion Wishlist — Built Out

Every excursion on this list is achievable from all three properties. Arenal Volcano and a proper coffee tour require an overnight or long day trip (4–5 hrs from Guanacaste, or a short domestic flight). Everything else — waterfall, kayaking, ATV — is within 30–90 min. A rental 4x4 unlocks the region on your own terms.
🌊
Water & Kayaking
Bioluminescent Night Kayak
Paquera Bay · Evening · ~$65/person

Paddle after dark while bioluminescent algae light up the water around your kayak — one of the few year-round spots on earth for this. Tours run 5:45–7:30 pm. Group size capped at 10; you could book the whole boat privately.

Year-Round ~$65/person 2 hrs Evening
Tortuga Island Snorkel Cruise
Nicoya Gulf · Full day · ~$85–110/person

Boat cruise to Isla Tortuga — volcanic reef snorkeling with angelfish, moray eels, and spotted eagle rays. White sand beach time and fresh fruit included. One of the best full-day excursions from the Nicoya Peninsula.

Top Rated ~$85–110/person Full day
Lake Arenal Kayak
La Fortuna · Combine with Volcano Day

Costa Rica's largest lake at the foot of Arenal Volcano — kayaking or SUP with the volcano as your backdrop. Best combined with the Arenal overnight. Sunset boat cruises also available.

Combine w/ Volcano ~$60/person Half day
Private Sportfishing Charter
Pacific Coast · 4–8 hrs · ~$600–900 whole boat

Private inshore charter targeting yellowfin tuna, mahi-mahi, roosterfish, and wahoo. Look for dolphins and whales on the way out — common in August. Split 4 ways, very reasonable.

Private Charter ~$150–225pp split 4 Morning

Waterfalls & Jungle
Montezuma Waterfalls
Montezuma Village · 45 min · Free entry

Three cascading falls with natural swimming pools — the most famous waterfall on the Nicoya Peninsula. A 1 km jungle hike from the road. Climb to upper pools for 25m cliff jumping. The road trip itself is half the experience: river crossings, mountaintop ocean views, deserted beaches.

Bucket List Free (~$1.50 upper) Half day Bring water shoes
La Fortuna Waterfall
La Fortuna · Combine with Volcano

A dramatic 70m cascade into a crystal-clear pool at the base of Arenal Volcano. 500 steps down (and back up). Best combined with the Arenal day trip — combo tours include the volcano hike, hanging bridges, and hot springs all in one day.

Combine w/ Volcano ~$20 entry Full day
Canopy Zip-Line
Cabo Blanco / Rincón de la Vieja · ~$55–75/person

Nearly a mile of zip-line through jungle canopy bordering Costa Rica's first protected reserve. 11 platforms high in the trees, ocean views, regular howler monkey sightings. Planet Hollywood guests have direct access to the Rincón de la Vieja adventure centre.

~$55–75/person 2–3 hrs All levels
Wildlife Refuge Guided Tour
Curú / Cabo Blanco · Private · ~$80/person

Private guided tour with a certified naturalist through secluded beach trails — howler monkeys, scarlet macaws, iguanas, coatis. Fully customizable. Pairs perfectly with the bioluminescent night kayak for a full nature day.

Private Tour ~$80/person Half day
☕
Coffee Tour
Don Juan Coffee & Chocolate Tour
La Fortuna · Best combined with Arenal

Hands-on farm experience covering the full bean-to-cup journey — plus cacao and sugar cane. The Arenal highlands produce some of Costa Rica's finest coffee. Guided tasting of multiple roasts. Best positioned as part of the Arenal overnight so you're not making the trip twice.

Best Combo w/ Volcano ~$35–50/person 2–3 hrs
Café Monteverde Cloud Forest Tour
Monteverde · 5 hrs from Guanacaste

Generations-old coffee farm in the misty cloud forest — volcanic soil, high altitude, family traditions over a century old. One of the most atmospheric coffee experiences in the country. Best as a Monteverde stopover rather than a standalone day trip from the coast.

Best: Monteverde stopover ~$30/person 1.5 hrs tour

Volcano
Arenal Volcano — Full Day or Overnight ✦ Signature Trip
La Fortuna · ~3.5–4 hrs drive or short domestic flight from Guanacaste

Costa Rica's most iconic volcano. Guided rainforest walks along lava flows, hanging bridges through the canopy, La Fortuna Waterfall, Don Juan coffee tour, and the Tabacon Hot Springs (20+ thermal pools volcano-fed, lit up at night). All doable in one big day or a comfortable overnight.

Getting there from all three resorts: Drive ~3.5–4 hrs, or take a short domestic flight from Liberia (LIR) to La Fortuna — roughly 40 min. Flying is strongly recommended for a day trip without losing two days to the road.

Signature Experience Domestic flight ~$150–200pp each way Drive: 3.5–4 hrs from Guanacaste Best as overnight Hot springs incl. in most tours
Arenal Base Hike
Arenal National Park · Guided · ~$50/person

Guided rainforest hike along old lava flows with a naturalist guide. Mostly flat to moderate. Wildlife spotting, flora education, volcano history. Go in the morning before clouds roll in.

~$50/person 3–4 hrs Morning
Tabacon Hot Springs
La Fortuna · Volcano-fed thermal pools

20+ naturally flowing thermal mineral springs at the base of Arenal — pools from 22–40°C. Available as a day or evening visitor pass. Most tour packages bundle with dinner. Genuinely unforgettable at night when the pools are lit up.

Don't Skip ~$70–90/person day pass Evening best

Car Rental

Get a 4x4 — especially in August.

August is green (rainy) season. Roads across Guanacaste can be unpaved, potholed, and flood after heavy afternoon rains. A standard sedan will limit where you can go. Every local operator recommends 4WD — the added cost is minimal vs. the freedom it provides for day trips to Montezuma, Cabo Blanco, and beyond.

Best Vehicle

4x4 SUV — Hyundai Creta or Suzuki Jimny. High clearance, automatic preferred for group comfort. Fits 4 adults plus luggage.

Where to Pick Up

Pick up at Liberia (LIR) airport — closest to all three properties. Adobe Rent a Car and Jumbo Car both operate there.

Cost in August

Green season = lowest rates of the year. ~$400–600 USD/week for a 4WD SUV. Book early — 4x4s go fast.

Adobe Rent a Car — Largest Fleet in CR Jumbo Car — No Hidden Fees Budget / Avis at LIR Airport ~$400–600 USD/week for 4WD SUV in August
